=== LLM SELECTION FOR LOCAL RAG ===

We evaluated 6 open-source LLMs for use as the generator and judge in our fully local RAG system. All models run via Ollama on M2 Mac (32GB RAM) and NVIDIA A100 (production server).

Criteria:
1. Answer quality on RAG tasks (1-5 scale, human eval)
2. Instruction following (JSON output reliability)
3. Tokens/second throughput on local hardware
4. Context window size
5. License compatibility

Models Evaluated:
- llama3.2:3b (Meta, Apache 2.0)
- llama3.2:7b (Meta, Apache 2.0)  [NOTE: "llama3.2" in Ollama = 3B model]
- mistral:7b (Mistral AI, Apache 2.0)
- phi3:mini (Microsoft, MIT)
- gemma2:2b (Google, Gemma ToS)
- qwen2.5:7b (Alibaba, Apache 2.0)

=== JUDGE CALIBRATION RESULTS ===

We compared LLM judge scores against human expert scores on 100 (query, context, answer) triples.

Judge model     | Pearson r vs human | Mean Abs Error | False high rate
llama3.2 (t=0) |      0.79          |    0.09        |    12%
mistral (t=0)  |      0.74          |    0.12        |    18%
phi3 (t=0)     |      0.71          |    0.14        |    21%

llama3.2 at temperature=0 is our recommended judge. The 0.79 Pearson correlation with human scores is acceptable for automated evaluation. We note that judges tend to be overconfident (false high rate) — keep this in mind when interpreting scores > 0.9.

=== TEMPERATURE SENSITIVITY ===

We ran the generator at different temperatures on 50 queries and measured faithfulness:

Temperature | Faithfulness | Diversity | Consistency (same query x3)
0.0         |    0.82      |   Low     |    High (0.95 similarity)
0.3         |    0.80      |   Med     |    Med  (0.87 similarity)
0.7         |    0.74      |   High    |    Low  (0.71 similarity)
1.0         |    0.65      |   Very High |  Very Low (0.58 similarity)

Findings:
- For the judge: always temperature=0 (reproducibility is paramount)
- For the generator: temperature=0.1 (slightly more natural responses than 0.0, still faithful)

=== CONTEXT WINDOW UTILIZATION ===

Tested how many chunks can be passed to the generator before quality degrades:

Chunks passed | Avg faithfulness | Avg answer length
1             |    0.91          |    82 tokens
3             |    0.87          |   134 tokens
5             |    0.84          |   189 tokens
10            |    0.78          |   247 tokens

Recommendation: Pass 3-5 most relevant chunks. More chunks add noise without improving answer quality for our query distribution.

=== OLLAMA DEPLOYMENT NOTES ===

1. Model loading: First inference has ~2s overhead (model load). Subsequent calls are fast.
2. Concurrency: Ollama handles one request at a time by default. For parallel evaluation, set OLLAMA_NUM_PARALLEL=4.
3. Memory: llama3.2 (3B) uses ~3.8GB RAM. nomic-embed-text uses ~270MB. Both fit comfortably on 8GB RAM.
4. GPU offload: Ollama auto-detects CUDA/Metal and offloads layers to GPU automatically.
5. Timeouts: Set generous timeouts (120s) for slow queries or cold starts.
